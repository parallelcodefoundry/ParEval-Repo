This translation uses OpenMP's `target teams distribute parallel for` directive to offload the execution of the `print_profile` function to a team of threads, allowing it to take advantage of multi-core processors. The `private` clause ensures that the variables in question (`profile` and `in`) are not shared with other threads, while the `distribute` clause distributes the work among all available threads. This can significantly improve performance by utilizing multiple cores and reducing thread contention.
