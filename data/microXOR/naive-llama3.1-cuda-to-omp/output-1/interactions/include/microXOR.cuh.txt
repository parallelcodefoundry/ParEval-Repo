Note that we've made the following changes:
- Renamed `microXOR.cuh` to `microXOR.hpp` and changed all occurrences of `.cu` to `.cpp`.
- Added `#include <omp.h>` for OpenMP support.
- Changed the function definition from `__global__` (a CUDA kernel) to a regular C++ function. Since this is no longer a GPU kernel, we need to call it explicitly in our main function.
