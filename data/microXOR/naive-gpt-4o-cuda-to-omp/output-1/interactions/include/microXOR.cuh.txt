### Explanation:

1. **Header Guards**: The header guard macro name has been updated to `MICROXOR_HPP` to reflect the change from `.cuh` to `.hpp`.

2. **Include Directives**: 
   - Removed `#include <cuda_runtime.h>` since CUDA-specific headers are not needed in OpenMP.
   - Added `#include <omp.h>` to include OpenMP functionalities.

3. **Function Declaration**: The `cellsXOR` function is now declared as a regular C++ function, as OpenMP does not require special syntax for function declarations. The `__global__` keyword is removed because it is specific to CUDA.
